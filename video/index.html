“Bad Habit” OPB. Ben Platt — The MSU Accafellas

During my time as the President of the MSU Accafellas, we were asked by Atlantic Records to arrange, record, and film a cover of Ben Platt’s new single “Bad Habit.” This is the product of my direction and editing, and my colleague’s arrangement, choreography, and music direction.

This project was completed entirely with Adobe Premiere Pro, shot on entry-level DSLR cameras.

“Urbandale Farm - 4th Annual Harvest Gala” — MSU Center for Community and Economic Development

For my internship at the MSU CCED, I was responsible for conducting the direction, production, and editing of an interview with a local urban farm called Urbandale in regards to their 4th annual Harvest Gala.

This project was completed entirely with Adobe Premiere Pro, shot on entry-level DSLR cameras.

“NewYorkSoul - Jon Bellion”

Continuing my passion for music, my friends and I decided to do a cover of Jon Bellion’s “NewYorkSoul”. I was responsible for the direction and editing, and the audio was recorded and produced by Daniel Dade.

This project was completed entirely with Adobe Premiere Pro, shot on entry-level DSLR cameras.
