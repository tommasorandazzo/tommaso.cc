A little about me

A long time ago, in a small town not too far away...

That’s where I come in! Hello, my name is Tommaso Randazzo and I’m a Web Developer & Designer, Graphic Designer, Social Media Manager, and singer from Michigan.

I’m currently pursuing a degree in Media and Information at Michigan State University where I am focusing on Human-Centered technology. That’s a fancy way to say I studied development, design, and user-experience.

I’m excited to graduate in August 2020 and delve into the world of development, design, and content creation!
